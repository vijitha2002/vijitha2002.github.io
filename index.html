JEWELHUT

Jewellery refers to decorative items worn for personal adornment, such as necklaces, rings, bracelets, earrings, and brooches. It has been a part of human culture for thousands of years, often symbolizing wealth, status, beauty, and spirituality. Jewellery can be made from a wide variety of materials, including precious metals like gold and silver, gemstones, beads, and even natural elements like shells or wood.
